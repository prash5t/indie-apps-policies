Privacy Policy

VParty - Vibrating Party · com.awarself.vparty · last updated 2026-05-04

VParty - Vibrating Party is built by awarself. We don't sell your data, we don't track you across apps, and we don't run any in-app purchases. This page lists exactly what we collect and why.

What we collect

  • Google account email (used internally only, never displayed to other users)
  • Display name (chosen by user, shown to others)
  • Avatar selection (one of 30 bundled assets)
  • Anonymous gameplay events (taps, drag, sessions joined)
  • Coin balance and transaction log
  • Crash logs (Firebase Crashlytics)

Third-party services

The app integrates these services. Each receives only the minimum data needed for its function.

  • Firebase Authentication (Google Sign-In)
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Realtime Database
  • Firebase Remote Config
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Mixpanel (behavioral analytics)
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob: banner, interstitial, rewarded)

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data to advertisers or anyone else.
  • We don't track you across other apps or websites.
  • We have no in-app purchases — there are no payment systems involved at all.
  • We don't share your personal entries with anyone.

Your data, your control

  • Sign out: clears your session locally.
  • Delete account: available in-app under Settings → Delete Account, or by emailing awarself@gmail.com. See Account Deletion for details.
  • Export your data: request a JSON dump by emailing awarself@gmail.com; we'll send it within 30 days.

Retention

Your data persists while your account is active. After deletion, anonymized crash logs are retained for 90 days for stability investigation, then permanently removed. All other data is deleted immediately on request.

Children

VParty - Vibrating Party is intended for users aged 13 and over. We don't knowingly collect data from children below this age.

Changes to this policy

If we change how we collect or use data, we'll update this page and bump the "last updated" date. Material changes will also be surfaced inside the app.

Contact

Questions, concerns, or data requests: awarself@gmail.com.
