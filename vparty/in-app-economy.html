In-App Economy

VParty - Vibrating Party · com.awarself.vparty · last updated 2026-05-07

VParty - Vibrating Party has no in-app purchases. You can't buy Sparks or anything else with real money. There are no subscriptions, no payment systems, and no surprise bills — ever.

How Sparks work

Sparks are VParty - Vibrating Party's in-app currency. They unlock optional features and convenience inside the app. They have no real-world value, can't be exchanged for money, and can't be transferred between accounts.

How you earn Sparks

Complete a 60s performer turn = 30 Sparks. Watch a rewarded ad = 25 Sparks. Daily check-in on Home = 10 Sparks. First LAN session = 50 Sparks one-time.

How you spend Sparks

Sound pad packs (200 Sparks). Avatar skins (150 Sparks). Light color palettes (80 Sparks). Haptic signature macros (120 Sparks). Default pack always free.

What Sparks are not

  • Not a currency — Sparks have no monetary value.
  • Not transferable — you can't send them to other users.
  • Not refundable — since you didn't pay for them, there's nothing to refund.
  • Not necessary — core features of VParty - Vibrating Party don't require Sparks. They unlock conveniences and optional content only.

Account closure and Sparks

If you delete your account, your Sparks balance is permanently erased along with the rest of your data. See Account Deletion.

Changes to the economy

We may adjust earn or spend rates over time based on actual app usage. If we change the economy materially, the change will be surfaced in-app and reflected on this page.
