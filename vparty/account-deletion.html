Account Deletion

VParty - Vibrating Party · com.awarself.vparty · last updated 2026-05-04

You can permanently delete your VParty - Vibrating Party account, your data, and your sign-in. There are two ways:

Option 1 — In-app (preferred)

  1. Open VParty - Vibrating Party.
  2. Go to Settings → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm twice (we ask twice on purpose so it's never an accidental tap).
  4. Done — your account and all data are removed immediately.

Option 2 — Email

If you can't access the app, email awarself@gmail.com from the address linked to your account. We'll process the request within 30 days and confirm by reply.

What gets deleted

  • Your sign-in (Google authentication record)
  • All your in-app data (entries, settings, preferences, history)
  • Your in-app Sparks balance and transaction history
  • Push notification subscriptions for your account
  • Any cached data on the device

What is briefly retained

Anonymized crash logs are kept for 90 days after deletion so we can investigate stability issues. These contain no personal identifiers and are deleted automatically. Nothing else is retained.

Irreversible

Deletion can't be undone. If you sign back in later, it will be a brand-new account with no history.
