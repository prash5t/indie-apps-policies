In-App Economy

Kpop Bias Tracker: Stan Card · awarself

com.awarself.kpopbias · v1.0 — initial release · last updated 2026-05-17

Coins are earned inside the app — never purchased. There is no way to spend real money in this app. No payment information is ever collected. Everything you can unlock is reachable through normal play.

What coins are

Kpop Bias Tracker uses an in-app coin balance (🪙) to let you re-roll daily content and unlock cosmetic extras. You earn coins by using the app; you spend them on specific features. Your balance is tied to your account and syncs across devices.

Starting balance

When you sign in for the first time, you receive a starting gift of 50 🪙 so you can explore right away.

How to earn coins

  • Daily check-in — earn coins once per 24 hours just by opening the app. The reward is the same whether you play for two minutes or two hours.
  • Watching an optional rewarded ad — watch a short, opt-in ad to earn a coin bonus. This is always your choice; you are never forced to watch an ad.
  • Winning a Bias Battle — earn coins each time you win a matchup.
  • Participating in a Bias Battle — earn a smaller amount just for entering a battle, even if you lose. Showing up counts.

Exact coin amounts are set via remote configuration and may be tuned over time, but the earn methods above are always available.

How to spend coins

  • Re-rolling your Daily Bias Pull — not the bias you were hoping for today? Spend coins to spin again and get a different card.
  • Re-rolling a lore card — spend coins to generate a fresh piece of lore for your bias if the first one doesn’t feel right.
  • Unlocking a premium card theme — one-time cost per theme. Once unlocked, the theme is yours permanently on your account.
  • Playing an extra Bias Battle after the daily limit — spend coins to get one more match when you’ve hit the daily cap.

No purchases, ever

There is no in-app purchase system and no way to buy coins with real money. No payment or billing screen will ever appear. If you see a third-party service offering Kpop Bias Tracker coins for sale, it is not affiliated with awarself and not legitimate.

What coins cannot do

  • Coins have no real-world monetary value.
  • Coins cannot be transferred between accounts.
  • Coins cannot be converted to anything outside the app.
  • Your coin balance is not visible to other users and is not part of any ranking or leaderboard.

What happens to coins when you delete your account

Your coin balance is permanently deleted along with your account. Coins have no monetary value and cannot be refunded or transferred. See Account Deletion for the full deletion details.
