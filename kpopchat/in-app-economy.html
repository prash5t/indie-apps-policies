In-App Economy

KpopChat · com.awarself.kpopchat · last updated 2026-05-11

KpopChat has no in-app purchases. You can't buy coins or anything else with real money. There are no subscriptions, no payment screens, and no surprise charges — ever. KpopChat is funded entirely by ads.

How coins work

Coins are KpopChat's in-app currency. They let you keep chatting longer and unlock new AI friends. They have no real-world monetary value, can't be exchanged for money, and can't be transferred between accounts.

How you earn coins

Watch a rewarded ad to earn coins. The rewarded ad is always optional — you choose when to watch one. Exact coin amounts per ad and exact costs per action are set via our configuration and may be tuned over time; they are always shown to you inside the app before you commit.

How you spend coins

Coins are spent to unlock extra messages in an ongoing chat or to unlock a new AI K-pop fan friend. You can always see your current balance and the cost of any action before confirming.

What coins are not

  • Not money — coins have no monetary or cash value.
  • Not purchasable — there is no way to buy coins with real money, ever.
  • Not transferable — you can't send coins to other accounts.
  • Not refundable — since you didn't pay for them, there's nothing to refund.

Account closure and coins

If you delete your account, your coin balance and full coin transaction history are permanently erased along with the rest of your data. See Account Deletion.

Changes to the economy

We may adjust earn or spend rates based on how the app is actually used. Any changes are reflected inside the app and on this page.
